Bio

I am a Staff Android Software Engineer developing Android & iOS core feature libraries for the App Platforms team at The New York Times in Austin, TX. When I'm not writing code, I'm usually training my three dogs. You can find more information on my past talks and work on Android and iOS at https://github.com/laurenyew.

After discovering my passion for native mobile app development, I have worked extensively in developing and architecting both Android and iOS mobile apps with both enterprise and client features. I have experience developing in Kotlin, Swift, Java, and Objective-C. I enjoy learning and employing new technologies and techniques to every problem and working in team environments.
